# Sample Prompts

- `what is the weather in Lviv right now?`
- `how is the weather in Berlin, Germany right now?`
- `tell me the current weather in Paris`
- `what is the temperature in Uzhhorod?`
- `compare weather in Berlin and Paris`
- `what is the weather in Springfield?`

Notes:

- `Springfield` is intentionally ambiguous and demonstrates the current first-result behavior.
- Comparative prompts may trigger multiple tool calls depending on model behavior.
